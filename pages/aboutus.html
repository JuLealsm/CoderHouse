ABOUT US

Our Journey - 2015

SafeBox was founded in 2015 by four visionaries passionate about technology and digital security. Inspired by the exponential growth of cyber threats and the need to protect businesses and individuals in an increasingly connected world, the founders combined their expertise to create a company committed to innovation and reliability. From the beginning, SafeBox’s mission was clear: to provide advanced cybersecurity solutions that ensure peace of mind and protection for businesses of all sizes.

Our Journey - 2017

In just two years since its founding, SafeBox has achieved remarkable success in the cybersecurity industry, standing out for its innovative solutions and commitment to data protection. This rapid growth has allowed the company to triple the size of its team, bringing together talented professionals who are passionate about technology and digital security. With a strengthened team, SafeBox has expanded its ability to serve customers and address increasingly complex challenges in the connected world.

Our Journey - 2019

In 2019, SafeBox launched its awareness program through workshops, with the goal of helping the community better understand cyber threats and how to protect themselves from them. This program was a response to the growing need for cybersecurity education, offering practical and informative sessions for businesses and individuals. SafeBox was dedicated to sharing its knowledge and experience, empowering people to implement more effective security practices in their daily lives. In addition to raising awareness, the workshops also provided an opportunity to strengthen the company’s relationship with the community.

Our Journey - 2021

In 2021, SafeBox was recognized with a significant award for its contribution to the cybersecurity market. This award reflects years of hard work and innovation, solidifying the company as a reference in the industry. SafeBox has distinguished itself through its advanced solutions and commitment to protecting businesses and individuals against evolving cyber threats. The recognition comes as a result of our dedication to providing high-quality and constantly evolving security services.

Our Journey - 2024

This photo from our 2024 Christmas party captures a special moment of celebration and unity among all members of the SafeBox team. With over 20 people in attendance, this event symbolizes the significant growth of the company over the years and the strong connection between team members. In a relaxed and welcoming environment, everyone shared laughter, stories and the pride of being part of a company dedicated to innovation and digital protection.

OUR VISION

Safebox envisions a future where digital security is seamlessly integrated into everyday life, empowering individuals and organizations to thrive in an interconnected world. By harnessing cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning, Safebox aims to proactively identify and neutralize threats before they can cause harm. The company is committed to creating intuitive security solutions that prioritize user experience, ensuring that protection feels effortless rather than burdensome.
In addition to its innovative products, Safebox takes the initiative to provide free educational resources to the community, recognizing that knowledge is a vital component of effective cybersecurity. Through webinars, interactive workshops, and comprehensive online guides, the company equips users with the skills and awareness necessary to protect themselves against digital threats. By fostering a culture of security literacy, Safebox seeks to build a community of informed users who are empowered to navigate the complexities of the digital landscape safely.
